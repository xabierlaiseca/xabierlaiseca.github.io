This's who I am!

I'm Xabier Laiseca, a Java backend Developer from Bilbao. I studied Software Engineering BSc and MSc at the University of Deusto which gave me the opportunity of being part of the MORElab research group for almost 7 years.

In June 2012, I moved to London where I joined the Ocado Technology team. Since March 2013, I'm part of the Gamesys's Core-Platform. Initially I joined the Payments and Risk development team and then I moved to the newly created Platform Team where I'm mainly contributing to the continuous delivery pipeline implementation.
